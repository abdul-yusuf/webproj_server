About Me

Shamsudeen MD Yusuf BULAMAN ZAZZAU was born on the 19th day of May, 1983 in Igabi local government area of Kaduna state. He attended Sarki nursery school Abakpa Kaduna, then moved to Kaduna Capital School where he obtained his first school leaving certificate from 1988 to 1994. Yusuf obtained his Senior Secondary School certificate at Uncle Bado Memorial College in 2000.

In the year 2005 Bulaman Zazzau graduated from the prestigious Ahmadu Bello University Zaria where he obtained a bachelor of science in building and Masters in facility management and his Masters degree in International Affairs and Diplomacy (MIAD) from the department of political science and international studies in (year).

With over 15 years of experience in building and Construction industry, he is known as a goal oriented team leader who is passionate about empowering and creating opportunities for young people as part of his commitments to enhancing human capital development in Kaduna State.
